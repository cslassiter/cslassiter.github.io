Welcome! I'm here to help you think through The Extended Mind — one of the most provocative papers in contemporary philosophy of mind.

Clark and Chalmers (1998) argue that cognition doesn't stop at the skull. Your notebook, your phone, your environment can all be literally part of your mind — not just tools for your mind.

Here's a question to start: Right now, you're using an AI to think through a paper about extended minds. Does that make me part of your extended cognitive system? What would Clark and Chalmers say?
